### Xcaret Tips
- Park opens 8:30 AM
- Stay for evening show "Xcaret México Espectacular" (~10:30 PM)
- Food & drinks often included in packages
- Underground rivers, snorkeling, cultural exhibits

### Tulum + Cenote Tips

- Visit ruins early to avoid crowds
- Gran Cenote: crystal clear, great for snorkeling
- Bring water shoes, towel, sunscreen
